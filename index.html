NEGARA INDONESIA

Negara Kesatuan Republik Indonesia

merupakan negara kesatuan berbentuk republik, di mana pemerintahan pusat memiliki wewenang tertinggi, namun daerah memiliki otonomi dalam urusan pemerintahan dan wilayahnya tetap utuh di bawah satu negara. Negara ini berlandaskan Pancasila, mengutamakan kedaulatan rakyat melalui pemilihan umum, serta menjunjung tinggi prinsip Bhinneka Tunggal Ika untuk menjaga persatuan di tengah keragaman budaya, suku, dan bahasa.

about

Indonesian culture is defined by its incredible ethnic, linguistic, and religious diversity, with a national motto, Bhinneka Tunggal Ika (Unity in Diversity), reflecting its goal of harmony across hundreds of ethnic groups and traditions. Key characteristics include a strong emphasis on family and community, influenced by concepts like gotong royong (mutual assistance), and a focus on respect for elders and maintaining face. Its culture is a rich blend of indigenous customs and foreign influences from trade, particularly from India, the Middle East, and the West, which have shaped its arts, architecture, cuisine, and religious landscape, coexisting with ancient belief systems.
